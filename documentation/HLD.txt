Problem Statement
Marketing performance is measured by individual channels, but operators lack visibility into how all marketing efforts work together as a system, making it difficult to understand reinforcement, isolation, and overall system health.
Your Mission
Build a channel-agnostic system that evaluates marketing as an interconnected ecosystem, helping operators understand how different channels reinforce or undermine one another, where momentum is being created or lost, and which parts of the system are underperforming relative to their role.

OUR SOLUTION
Connect your existing marketing tools. We pull all the data, cross-reference everything, and show you:
1. What each channel actually did (verified performance through dual tracking: our custom pixel + Google Analytics 4)
2. How channels work together (system intelligence through visual network mapping)
3. What to do next (AI recommendations with financial impact)
Dual tracking: Our custom pixel + Google Analytics 4 cross-verification catches what each system misses, giving you 85-95% accuracy vs 60-70% from single sources. We verify conversions when both agree, expose platform over-attribution, and send only verified conversions back via CAPI so your ads optimize better.
System intelligence: Marketing isn't isolated channels‚Äîit's an ecosystem. We reveal synergies (Facebook + Email = 5x together), reinforcement (45% of Google searches follow Facebook ads), isolation (Instagram Ads in only 4% of journeys), and conflicts (contradictory messaging killing performance). Then AI tells you exactly how to coordinate combinations, cut isolated spend, and orchestrate your entire system instead of optimizing channels in silos.
